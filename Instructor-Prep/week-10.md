## Mon Jun 1 — Brevity & polish

### Brevity rules of thumb

- One idea per sentence; split **monster** sentences  
- Delete **vague** intensifiers (“very”, “clearly”, “interestingly”) unless data-backed  
- **Redundancy**: if Intro says it and Discussion repeats without added value, cut one  
- Methods: **step order** matches how you actually ran the analysis

### “Cut this first” list (discussion)

- Repeated **citation blocks** that don’t advance argument  
- Figure description that duplicates **axis labels**  
- **Three** ways of saying the same limitation—keep one
