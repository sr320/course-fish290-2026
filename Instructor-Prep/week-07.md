## Mon May 11 — Discussion section

### Teaching structure

1. **Headline findings** (no new stats—mirror Results)  
2. **Interpretation** in context of literature (cite)  
3. **Limitations** (measurement, scope, confounders)  
4. **Implications / next steps** (careful—don’t oversell)

### Limitations — class prompt

“What is a **fair** limitation vs. what **undermines** the whole study?”
