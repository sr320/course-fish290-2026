## Mon May 18 — Abstracts

### Function (one slide)

Abstract = **standalone advertisement**: context, gap, what you did, main finding, significance—in **journal word limit** (often 150–300 words; set a class limit).

### In-class abstract peer-edit (10–15 min pairs)

Swap drafts; reader answers only:

1. **First sentence**: could a non-specialist tell *why* this matters?  
2. **Methods**: one phrase—is the study design clear?  
3. **Results**: is the **main** outcome stated with magnitude/direction (not just “we analyzed”)?  
4. **Last sentence**: implication without overclaim?
