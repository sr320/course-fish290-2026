## Mon May 4 — Results text & mock results

### Mock results discussion — facilitator prompts

- Where does **description** end and **interpretation** begin? (Flag leakage into Discussion.)  
- Is every **claim** tied to a **figure/table/stat**?  
- Order: broad → specific or figure-by-figure—what aids *your* reader?

### Non-significant results — talking points

- Non-significance **is a result**; report estimate + CI/SE if available.  
- Avoid “prove no effect”; use **“no detectable difference”** with methods transparent.  
- Connect to **limitations** (sample size, noise) without sounding defensive.
