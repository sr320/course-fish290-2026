## Wed Apr 1 — Scientific method & ideas

### Discussion anchors (readings)

- **Storytelling**: Where is the “story” in a paper—without becoming fiction?
- **Reading papers**: What do you do when Results don’t make sense on first read? What is a productive first pass vs. deep read?

### In-class flow suggestions

1. Hear student questions/ideas; categorize on board: **too broad / feasible / needs data**.
2. Short mini-lecture: variations on how science is done (hypothesis-driven, exploratory, observational, etc.).
3. Introduce **next step**: narrowing question + thinking about **data you can actually access**.
